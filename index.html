The Opportunity

Why Graduate to GREAT — Why Now

The Philippines faces a critical crossroads. Over 12.7 million jobs are exposed to Generative AI disruption. Yet 50% of businesses cite the lack of digital skills as their #1 barrier to growth. The gap between what graduates know and what employers need has never been wider.


Graduate to GREAT bridges that gap — with radical clarity, practical AI skills, and the professional networks that matter.
